User Guide

Place your hand in front of the camera to start interacting with the virtual canvas.

  • Draw: Raise your index finger. The tip of your finger acts as your brush.
  • Erase (Gesture): Use the erase mode by forming the appropriate gesture (e.g., two fingers up or an open hand, depending on your setup).
  • Change Color: Hover your virtual cursor over the color buttons located in the on-screen toolbar.
  • Toggle Eraser (Button): Touch the eraser icon in the virtual toolbar to switch to eraser mode.
  • Clear All: Touch the "Clear" button in the toolbar to wipe the entire canvas.
Tip: Ensure you have good lighting in your room so that the hand tracking remains optimal and smooth.
